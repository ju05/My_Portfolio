My main projects

Recipes Searcher and Planner

A meal planner and searcher with special login for either regular users or chefs/restaurants.
Made using Google OAuth 2.0 authentication through Django allauth.
The users can search for recipes and add them to their calendar using the Google Calendar API.

Hotel Management

A website with two interfaces, one for visitors and another for staff.
The visitor interface allows login, contact with the hotel, checking availability, and making a booking.
The project was made using Django and PostgreSQL, allowing for staff management.
Staff can make reservations for users, delete and edit bookings, and also add new rooms.
